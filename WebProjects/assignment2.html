What is Gel X?

Gel-X is a "soft extension," lengthening your nails while still allowing the flexible feeling. They appear as clear press-on extensions coming in a variety of nail shapes. It is made of gel polish and is applied directly on the natural nail, covering the entire nail from cuticle to free edge. When applied they can last from 3-4 weeks minimum while maximum can vary from 4-9 weeks depending on how they are taken care of. Below is a video of how to apply the Gel X extensions.

How To Book

When booking please have a date in mind and design. Through direct message give the following information: length, shape and design in mind (or send an inspiration picture) along with the day and time looking to book. Each set time varies so setting aside 2-3 hours is ideal, do not book if you have plans an hour into the appointment!

Please come with bare nails as well, let me know if you need a soak off. Soak offs are an additional $10 with service and $15 without service. There is also a 15-minute grace period, after 15 minutes the appointment is cancelled.
